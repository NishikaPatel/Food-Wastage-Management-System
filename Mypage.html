Food waste is a bigger problem than many people realize.

•In fact, nearly one-third of all food produced in the world is discarded or wasted for various reasons. That equates to nearly 1.3 billion tons every year.

•So instead of wasting the food it should be better donated to needy because nearly about 1000 of needy people sleep without food due to lack of money n availability of food.

•So don't waste food,its like wasting one of the most precios thing given by god to us.

•We aim at collecting excessive food from Hotels, family /public functions, pack them neatly and provide them to the needy homeless people around us.

•No food waste is a mission to end food waste and hunger to make the “World Hunger Free”.
